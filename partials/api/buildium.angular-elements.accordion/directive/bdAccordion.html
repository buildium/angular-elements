bdAccordion

  1. - directive in module buildium.angular-elements.accordion

Collapsible content panels with configurable headings and triggers

Directive Info

  • This directive executes at priority level 0.

Usage

  • as element: 
    <bd-accordion
  single-section-only="Boolean">
...
</bd-accordion>

Arguments

Param Type Details
singleSectionOnly
(optional)
 Boolean

whether to restrict accordion to one section open at a time

(default: false)

Examples

<section class="page-content">
    <bd-accordion>
        <bd-accordion-section heading="Panel 1">
            Panel 1
        </bd-accordion-section>
        <bd-accordion-section heading="Panel 2">
            Panel 2
        </bd-accordion-section>
    </bd-accordion>

    <hr>
    <label class="form__label">single section only</label>
    <bd-accordion single-section-only="true">
        <bd-accordion-section heading="Panel 1">
            Panel 1
        </bd-accordion-section>
        <bd-accordion-section heading="Panel 2" is-open="false">
            Panel 2
        </bd-accordion-section>
    </bd-accordion>
</section>
